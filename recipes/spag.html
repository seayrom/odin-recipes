Spag

Description

The source of this meal is unknown but what is known is that it is very delicious.

It is a simple meal from spaghetti and really nothing else.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Break spaghetti into boiling water
  3. Wait to cook
  4. Sieve remaining water
  5. Serve hot with stew and protein source of choice
